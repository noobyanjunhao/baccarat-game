Welcome to Baccarat!

Baccarat is a card game where you bet on which hand will have a total closest to 9: the Player's hand or the Banker's hand.

Game Rules:

  • Card values: Aces = 1, 2-9 = face value, 10s and face cards = 0
  • Only the last digit of the total matters (e.g., a total of 15 is worth 5)
  • A "natural" 8 or 9 with the first two cards wins automatically
  • Player draws a third card if their total is 0-5, stands on 6-7
  • Banker's third card draw depends on their total and Player's third card
  • Payouts: Player bet pays 1:1, Banker bet pays 1:1 (minus 5% commission), Tie pays 8:1
